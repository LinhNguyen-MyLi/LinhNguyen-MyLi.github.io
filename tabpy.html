Final assignment SpaceX on IBM

In this capstone project, our goal is to predict the successful landing of the Falcon 9 first stage. SpaceX prominently features Falcon 9 rocket launches on its website, offering them at a cost of 62 million dollars, while other providers charge upwards of 165 million dollars for similar launches. A significant portion of the cost savings lies in SpaceX's ability to reuse the first stage of the rocket. Therefore, by determining the likelihood of a successful first-stage landing, we can estimate the overall cost of a launch. This valuable information can be particularly useful if another company wishes to compete with SpaceX in bidding for rocket launch contracts.
